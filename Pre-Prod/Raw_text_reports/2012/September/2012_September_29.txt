Orca Sightings for September 29, 2012
============================================================

Entry 1:
Six or seven transient orcas
, including two youngsters, near
Constance Bank, Victoria, around
2:15 pm
, traveling in a tight group. The weather was benign with calm seas and late summer sunshine - the season's not over yet!
Sandra Pollard, Freeland, Whidbey Island (Naturalist on 'Mystic Sea')
