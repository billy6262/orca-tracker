Orca Sightings for January 13, 2009
============================================================

Entry 1:
Erin Falcone and Greg Schorr of Cascadia Research conducted a survey for marine mammals out of
Westport, WA
. During the course of the survey, they encountered and photo- identified a
blue whale
near Grays Harbor Canyon. In addition, we spotted several
fin whales
and southbound
gray whales
, among others. More information and photos can be found at the Cascadia website.
Erin Falcone and Greg Schorr, Cascadia Research, Olympia, WA
