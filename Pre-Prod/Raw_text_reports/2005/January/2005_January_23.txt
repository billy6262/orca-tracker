*
Jim Day of the fishing vessel Barbara Lee called this afternoon with a report of a pod of ~10
orcas
near Bird Island, about 5 miles off Brookings, OR at 4 pm. They were frolicking & heading north, and were at 24 fathoms. He saw 3 males, 4 females & 3 calves.
