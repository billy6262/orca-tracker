Orca Sightings for October 18, 2005
============================================================

Entry 1:
Dave Ellifrit of the Center for Whale Research saw a few
orcas
(L2's) off Pt. Wilson, north of Port Townsend at
9 am
, looking like they were heading north in foggy conditions. At
10:30 calls were heard off Pt. Partridge. Around noon he was with members of L pod north of Protection Island, with the whales heading west to Dungeness at 1 pm. At 4 pm they were between Port Angeles & Victoria, heading west toward Race Rocks.
(report from Dave Ellifrit, Center for Whale Research & Scott Veirs, Beam Reach)
