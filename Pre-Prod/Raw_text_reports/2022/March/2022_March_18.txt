Entry 1:
San Juan Islands (T90s, T123s) -
After having to wait longer than we expected, we finally had our first Bigg's killer whale research encounter of 2022! We met up with the
T90s and T123s in Rosario Strait
, where all 8 whales were traveling north together in a tight group. Pictured here are adult male
T123A Stanley and his youngest sister, 3.5-year-old T123D Darcy
. -Orca Behavior Institute
