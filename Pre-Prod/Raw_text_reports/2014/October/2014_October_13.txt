Just saw a pod of
4 or 5 orcas from the ferry just east of the Lopez Ferry Terminal
. Looked like they were feeding. They made a kill off the point just east of the marina just east of the lopez ferry dock. Watched for 10 mins or so from ferry. Weren't going anywhere. Saw multiple spyhops and full breaches. - Charyn Pfeuffer
