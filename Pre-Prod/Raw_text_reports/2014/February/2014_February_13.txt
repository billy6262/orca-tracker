Orca Sightings for February 13, 2014
============================================================

Entry 1:
We observed
8 Orcas heading south mid inlet from our shop at the old Bamberton Cement plant near Mill Bay (Saanich Inlet) around
3:30 pm
. There seemed to be various sizes, young and old. They were a fair distance out so not much else to report. Took photos with my iphone but so small as to be barely visible. - James Harcourt as
