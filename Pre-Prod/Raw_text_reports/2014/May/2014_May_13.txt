Orca Sightings for May 13, 2014
============================================================

Entry 1:
7:15 PM - Orca Pod near Depoe Bay Oregon
. I saw a pod of Orca near the first buoy in Depoe Bay. They were spyhopping, breaching and feeding on a bait ball. There were approximately 5 Orca with one large male. - Brent McWhirter
