Orca Sightings for December 8, 2003
============================================================

Entry 1:
Whales between Becher Bay and Beechey Head just after
1400
going west. (between Race Rocks and Sooke). 1 very large male and 12+ others including 2 calves. Their actions seemed to indicate residents.
Ron Bates
MMRG, Victoria BC
*
Dave Ellifrit of the Center for Whale Research found J pod just south of Active Pass at

12:15pm
, and left them at
1 pm
heading east in Active Pass. He ID'd 17 J pod whales, and said there was much socializing and talking amongst the whales.
