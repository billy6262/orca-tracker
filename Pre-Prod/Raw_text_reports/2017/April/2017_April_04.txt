..."Orcinus" arrived on scene about three quarters of a mile southeast of Gordon Head at the bottom of
Cordova Bay (8-10 miles north of Victoria BC) at 1241. T11 and T11A were separated by several hundred meters with T11A being inshore of and slightly ahead of T11
as they traveled north in forage mode. They were both taking very long, long dives of the 7-10 minute variety and coming up for their short dives separately. -See full summary report and photos at Center for Whale Research Encounter 28.
