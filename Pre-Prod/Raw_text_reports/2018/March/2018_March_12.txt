Orca Sightings for March 12, 2018

Entry 1:
Haro Strait -

7:08 p.m. - 4 for sure, maybe 5, orcas traveling leisurely to the N/NW between Pile Pt and Edwards Point
. One large male. Video taken with phone from shore. So wonderful! Haro Strait, San Juan Island. -Michelline Halliday
