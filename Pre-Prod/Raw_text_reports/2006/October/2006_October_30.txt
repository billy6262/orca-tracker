Orca Sightings for October 30, 2006
============================================================

Entry 1:
Rob Grennan from the Tugboat Pacific called in a report of 2 adult
orcas
heading south in Colvos Passage off Vashon Island, near Anderson Pt. (~mid-way down Vashon Island) at
2 pm
. They also sighted 3 orcas last week between West Pt. & Port Madison, also heading south - that sighting included at least one adult male.
