Orca Sightings for November 20, 2008
============================================================

Entry 1:
Morgan Hall called with a report of a
single male orca
seen around
1 PM
, off the coast of
California
, just north of Shelter Cove and about 8-9 miles south of
Cape Mendocino
. It was travelling slowly north about 1/4 mile offshore, among a group of porpoises.
