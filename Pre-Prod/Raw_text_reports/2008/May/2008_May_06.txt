Orca Sightings for May 6, 2008
============================================================

Entry 1:
Around

6:00 p.m
I looked out and saw a lone (
gray
) whale outside my window feeding in front of my house. I am on Witter Beach (SE Whidbey) and face directly east toward Hat Island and Everett. He hung around for quite awhile making his way south.
Sharon Wandler, Whidbey Island
