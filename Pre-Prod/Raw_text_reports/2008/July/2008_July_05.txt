Orca Sightings for July 5, 2008

Entry 1:
Orca Network received a call from Tina Dinzl- Pederson, Cama Beach Interpreter, relaying a report of a
gray whale
sighted off Cama Beach, west Camano Island, at

1:30 pm
heading SE. The whale was approx. 150- 200' from shore, but heading toward mid-channel.
