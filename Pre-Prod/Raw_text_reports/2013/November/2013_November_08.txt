Orca Sightings for November 8, 2013
============================================================

Entry 1:
I came across the
G2 and G27 matrilines (Northern Residents) foraging east of Race Rocks in the morning
. There was a group of
Pacific White-Sided dolphins
accompanying them. I last saw them
off of Sooke
west bound passing through a group of humpback whales. - Mark Malleson, Prince of Whales
*
Pacific White-Side dolphin were west of Race Rocks
following the
Northern Resident killer whales
out west. - Mark Malleson
