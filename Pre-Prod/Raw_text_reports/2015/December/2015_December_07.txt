Orca Sightings for December 7, 2015
============================================================

Entry 1:
12:50 p.m. -
Sidney - FH/Anacortes sailing. Just after crossing
Haro Strait, N of Roche
and nearing Spieden channel, Capt. announced the ferry was stopping for orcas.
At least 3, possibly up to 6 Ts with T19B and T18
traveling ENE fairly spread out. A few of them decided to swim alongside the ferry for a few minutes, giving everybody gorgeous flank views. -Ariel Yseth
(
ID by Melisa Pinnow, CWR
)
