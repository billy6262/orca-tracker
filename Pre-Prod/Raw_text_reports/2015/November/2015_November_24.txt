Orca Sightings for November 24, 2015
============================================================

Entry 1:
4:45 p.m. - Spotted four orcas swimming lazily in Pickering Passage northbound
on the west side of northern Harstine Island, south Puget Sound, one orca was very likely a male. He was trailing the other three orcas by 200-300m and was swimming in more of a circular pattern (feeding?). -Paul Dinnel
