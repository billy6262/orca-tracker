Orca Sightings for January 11, 2015
============================================================

Entry 1:
I came across the
entire Southern Resident killer whale community
in the afternoon
southwest of Race Rocks
. I left the lead animals at
1440 south of Sooke traveling out
the Strait with the ebb current. -Mark Malleson, Prince of Whales

Orcas
