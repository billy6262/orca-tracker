Orca Sightings for August 12, 2003
============================================================

Entry 1:
J and K pods came down Rosario St. early this morning, by
0845
they were already at Bird Rocks. They spent most of the day between the Salmon bank and Hein Bank area. Last I heard K's were going towards South Beach (S.San Juan Is.) J's were about 6 miles S. of Discovery Is. being erratic with some milling going on. This was about
1630
.
Ron Bates
MMRG, Victoria
