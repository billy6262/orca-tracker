Orca Sightings for September 13, 2019

Entry 1:
18:08 -
I was on the Blackball Ferry to
Victoria from Port Angeles and saw at least 2 humpbacks
. One breached several times for us!! They were east of the ferry lane. Seemed to be heading south! -Best, Kris Holley
