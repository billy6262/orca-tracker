Orca Sightings for November 30, 2009
============================================================

Entry 1:
Mark Kidraz of
San Mateo, CA
called Orca Network to report sighting a pod of
orcas
at
9 am
, off San Mateo, 40 miles so. of San Francisco, off the Pigeon Pt. Lighthouse, 1/4 mile offshore. There were 2 groups of 3 orcas, 6 total, including 1 adult male. They were heading north fast, no photos were taken.
