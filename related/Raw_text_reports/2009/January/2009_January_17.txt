Orca Sightings for January 17, 2009
============================================================

Entry 1:
Orca Network received a call from Tom Estes of California, reporting a sighting of 3-4 adult female orcas along with some calves working surface waters about 10-12 miles NW of
Pt. Arena CA
, at the head of the Navarro Canyon at about 300 fathoms, at 39.00.55 x 123.58.73. They were moving north gradually, then disappeared. They watched them for about 1/2 hour while trawling.
