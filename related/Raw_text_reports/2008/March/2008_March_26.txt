Orca Sightings for March 26, 2008
============================================================

Entry 1:
Fred Lundahl reported 4 or 5
gray whales
off Baby Island at
2 pm
, heading SE toward Langley, then at 3 pm reported the whales had arrived in Langley!
*
Monte Hughes of Mystic Sea Charters called at

12:45 pm
to report 2
grays
just south of Coupeville, & 2 more
grays
south of Lowell Pt, west Camano, all heading south.
*
Barbara Anderson of Camano Island called at

10:30 am
to report 1
gray
400 - 500 yards off the Camano side of Saratoga Passage across from Langley, heading north.
