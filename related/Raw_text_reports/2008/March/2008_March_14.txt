Orca Sightings for March 14, 2008
============================================================

Entry 1:
0845
- Bells Beach (NE of Langley, Whidbey) Two
Gray whales
feeding just beyond the crab pot buoys. One was Patch! I left after 20+ min of feeding. They were heading SE toward Langley.
Peggy Sullivan, Bells Beach, Whidbey Island
*
At

11:00 a.m.
Sighted 3
gray whales
about ½ mile north of the Langley Sea wall feeding about 100 yard off shore. After about 20 min they moved out into the channel and slowly headed south east. Last seen about ½ way between Camano and Sandy Point.
Ed Young, Whidbey Island Kayaking
