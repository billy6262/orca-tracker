Orca Sightings for January 14, 2008
============================================================

Entry 1:
Janet Cummings called a little after noon, to report that a pod of 8 - 12
orcas
had just passed 1.5 miles west of the Sekiu River in the Strait of Juan de Fuca, heading west at a fast pace. They were cruising among the kelp beds, 1 male was present in the pod.
*
Mary Jane Brown called at noon to report a pod of 8 - 9
orcas
off mile post 8 outside of Sekiu (assume they were also heading west, as in above report).
