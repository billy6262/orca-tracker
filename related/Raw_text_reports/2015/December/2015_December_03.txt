Orca Sightings for December 3, 2015
============================================================

Entry 1:
09:37 a.m. - A pod of eight orcas were seen from the ferry shortly after departing from Port Townsend to Keysone.
The whales were south of the ferry route, moving southward. -John Rogstad WSF
*

2:00 p.m. - Humpback whale, between Fauntleroy and Vashon Island traveling southbound.
-John Rogstad, WSF
