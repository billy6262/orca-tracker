Sunny skies returned as we headed out near Cattle Point (San Juan Island) today.
J-Pod
was about as spread out as I've seen, in groups of 3-5. Lots of feeding activity, and one of the younger whales appeared as if they wanted to set the record for most breaches in one minute.
John Boyd (JB)
Naturalist, San Juan Excursions
