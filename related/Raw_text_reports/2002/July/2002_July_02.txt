Orca Sightings for July 2, 2002
============================================================

Entry 1:
A number of Lpod going up and down between Hannah Hts. and False Bay, other Lpod whales going W. past Sooke. Not on scene but a report of the Lpod whales going towards Iceberg Pt. and J's and K's coming N.W. to meet them at 1611.
