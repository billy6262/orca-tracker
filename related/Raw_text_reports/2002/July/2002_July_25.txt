Orca Sightings for July 25, 2002
============================================================

Entry 1:
Odd day, at about 0750 whales reported going E. from the E. side of Active Pass. Near noon whales reported coming to East Pt. going S.W. Most of Lpod off San Juan Is. going N. at that time.
At 1519 a large number of Lpod whales between Salmon Bank and Hein bank turning N. J and Kpod nearing Lime Kiln going S.
Ron Bates,
MMRG, Victoria
