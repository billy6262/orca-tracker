Orca Sightings for May 17, 2002
============================================================

Entry 1:
Shane Aggergaard of Island Adventures called this morning to report orcas off Sucia Island at
8:30, moving west toward Boundary Pass. He also had a report from Roger Jones that 2 gray whales were feeding off Mission Beach, & have been there for 2 days.
