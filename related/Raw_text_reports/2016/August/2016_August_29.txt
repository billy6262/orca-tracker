11:30 a.m. - Orca inside Port Angeles
, WA. Harbor.
5 total
(2 small and 1 large juvenile, a female and possible male). They were here late morning today. Feeding at first, then casually left the harbor. There was one on the outside of the harbor all by itself. Wonderful morning watching the Orca in our harbor in Port Angeles, WA . I know it happens, but I have lived here 36 years and never seen them in our harbor before. So exciting! -Merri McDonald
