Species:
Orca

Transient
Members:

Direction:
west
Comments:

2:30 pm: A small pod ( one large male and about 7-8 females) just passed Gowlland Point, South Pender travelling toward Saturna. They were closer than we have seen all summer. They stopped and circled a small island of rocks, and we saw at least on seal taken...what an amazing sight. Diane McBain
