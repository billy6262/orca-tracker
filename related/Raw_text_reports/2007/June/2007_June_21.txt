Orca Sightings for June 21, 2007
============================================================

Entry 1:
Laurie Davidson called at

7:10 pm
to say they were boating & saw
orcas
spread out across Thatcher Pass and Rosario Strait. As the Washington State ferry Yakima came across the whales in Thatcher pass, it slowed down & stopped for 10 minutes - they were happy to see the care the Capt. took while the whales were in the area. But after the Yakima docked, the Hyak departed the Anacortes ferry dock, & steamed right over to and through the whales without slowing down as it neared them.
