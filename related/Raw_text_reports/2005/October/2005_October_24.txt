Orca Sightings for October 24, 2005
============================================================

Entry 1:
We received a call reporting 6
orcas
(possibly more, visibility not good) were sighted from the ferry at

5:25 pm
.
*
The Center for Whale Research reported
J pod (w/L57, L7 & L53)
heading west & L pod heading east in the strait of Juan de Fuca, meeting west of Race Rocks at about 3 pm. They met up & mingled, breaking into small mixed groups, then headed eastward.
