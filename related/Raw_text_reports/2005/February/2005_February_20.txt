Orca Sightings for February 20, 2005
============================================================

Entry 1:
Killer whales
off of Sisters Point (Lower Hood Canal) circled and dove around in front of our place on Sisters Point for a short while and then headed W, SW. This was at

5:35 PM
. There were four. It looked like two adults and two calves.
