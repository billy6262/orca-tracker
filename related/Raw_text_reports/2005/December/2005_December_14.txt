Orca Sightings for December 14, 2005

Entry 1:
11:00 am
a lone
Humpback
did two full body breaches at Chito Beach (seven miles west of Sekiu). He was heading north west on a line straight to Port Renfrew. The flukes had white spots on the underside, with the largest spots on both tips.
