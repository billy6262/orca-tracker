Orca Sightings for May 30, 2017
============================================================

Entry 1:
Puget Sound/Admiralty Inlet -
4:45 p.m. -
Kit Turner reports the pod is grouped up tightly, off the north end of
Lagoon Pt
, nearing Admiral's Cove, mid-channel, continuing north in Admiralty Inlet.

4:00 p.m. -
After Pat Scott's report I raced to the north side of
Bush Point and was able to see and photograph at least six Ts
as they cruised slowly a few hundred yards from shore past the boat ramp, then veered out toward midchannel. -Howard Garrett, Orca Network

3:53 p.m. - Now at Bush Point slowly Northbound
. -Christopher Hanke, Puget Sound Express

1:52 p.m. - Orca are passing Useless Bay

12:05 p.m. -
finally resighted they are further west from my previous report: approx
mile south of Scatchet Head/Cultus Bay

11:30 a.m. - I just got to Edmonds and see pod, including 2 adult males.
They are moving slowly north and/or kind of stalled. Currently approx 1 mile south of Possessiin Point. I just got here so not sure but looked to be resting and trending north. -Alisa Lemire Brooks
