Orca Sightings for October 27, 2014
============================================================

Entry 1:
So. Residents at Alden Bank
(between Bellingham and Vancouver) going North. - Capt. James Maya
*
Maia of WA State Ferries called at
9 am
to report a pod of
15 -20 orcas in Rosario Strait,
reported by the Elwha, as they were coming out of the San Juan Islands into the traffic lanes headed into Anacortes. The pod was traveling northbound.
